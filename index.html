Hi, I'm Sarah!

I'm an 18-year-old who discovered my passion for face painting at my little sister's 5th birthday party. What started as a fun activity has turned into a hobby I love sharing with others!

I'm not a professional artist, but I've practiced lots of kid-friendly designs that children absolutely love. I use only non-toxic, hypoallergenic face paints that are safe for sensitive skin and wash off easily with soap and water.
